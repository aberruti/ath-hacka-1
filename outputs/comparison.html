Key Takeaway

The custom-firm-voice skill transforms the output from an internally-oriented deal memo into a client-ready proposal with a distinctive, confident voice. The same underlying intelligence from the four specialists (Pricing, Legal, Technical Fit, Competitive Intel) is present in both — but the firm-voice version packages it as a document you could actually send to a client without editing. The coordinator synthesises the same inputs but writes for the reader who matters: the buyer.
